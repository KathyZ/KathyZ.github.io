About Queenly

Trisha Bantigue, CEO and co-founder
Kathy Zhou, CTO and co-founder

Queenly originated from the pageant industry--the founders saw a unique problem with observing thousands of girls experiencing trouble finding the perfect formal dress for their special night. Retail stores had limiting choices and unaffordable prices. They also saw that girls utilized unsafe platforms to transact and were often scammed. This was extremely disheartening for them, and so they finally decided it was time to create something better that all the girls deserved.

Trisha (right) and Kathy (left) first met when they both interned at a consumer analytics startup in San Francisco, CA in 2014. Both are passionate in constantly innovating and empowering those around them to be their best selves. Together, they've combined their love of fashion, pageants, supporting women and tech startups to found Queenly.
